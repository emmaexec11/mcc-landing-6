Full Ingredient List

Raspberry Powder
Adds a natural flush of color from real fruit, while offering skin-soothing antioxidants for a healthy glow.

Beet Root Powder
Provides rich, vibrant pigment from vegetables—helps tint lips and cheeks naturally without synthetic dyes.

Bilberry Extract
A fruit-derived antioxidant that helps protect skin and adds a subtle, naturally flattering hue.

Myrica Berry Wax
Plant-based wax that helps the stick hold its shape and glide on smoothly without heaviness or drag.

Candelilla Wax
Vegan alternative to beeswax that creates a protective barrier, locking in moisture while offering smooth texture.

Shea Butter
Deeply nourishing butter that softens skin, soothes dryness, and gives your skin a dewy, plump finish.

Coconut Oil
Lightweight hydration that melts into skin, supporting smooth application and conditioning benefits.

Jojoba Oil
Mimics your skin’s natural oils, balancing moisture while helping the tint absorb evenly and last longer.

Castor Oil
Adds a natural sheen and creamy texture, while locking in hydration for supple, soft lips and cheeks.

Olive Squalane
A luxurious, skin-identical hydrator that gives a silky feel, reduces fine lines, and adds a natural glow.

Vitamin A 
Supports skin renewal and smoothness—helps improve skin texture while wearing the tint.

Vitamin E 
A powerful antioxidant that helps protect your skin and extend the shelf life of natural oils.

Hyaluronic Acid
Binds moisture to the skin, leaving your lips and cheeks plump, hydrated, and never cakey.

Isoamyl Laurate
A 100% naturally derived ester that improves spreadability, giving the stick a soft, luxurious glide.

Cetearyl (from coconuts)
A coconut-derived emollient and texture enhancer that creates a silky-smooth, non-greasy finish.

Xanthan Gum
A natural stabilizer that helps hold the formula together for a consistent application every time.

Gum Arabic
Plant-based binder that helps the pigments stay in place and improves overall texture and feel.

Green Tea Extract
Rich in antioxidants, helps soothe irritated skin and calm redness—perfect for sensitive skin types.

Oxides
Safe, mineral-based colorants (like iron oxide) that provide long-lasting, stable tint without synthetic dyes.

Bamboo Silica
Natural source of silica that helps the tint stay in place longer, improving wear time without synthetic binders—supports smooth feel and a clean finish without mattifying.

Mineral Micas
Naturally sourced minerals that provide beautiful, buildable color and a subtle glow—these are the primary pigments used to create the full range of shades without synthetic dyes.

Frequently Asked Questions 

1. What exactly is a multicolour cream, and how do I use it?

Our multicolour cream is a 3-in-1 natural makeup balm designed for lips, cheeks, and eyes. Just swipe it on with clean fingers or a brush for instant colour and hydration. It blends seamlessly and is ideal for busy mornings, travel, or minimalist beauty lovers.

2. Are the pigments really natural?

Yes — every shade is made with real fruit and vegetable powders (like beetroot and bilberry) plus safe mineral micas and oxides. No synthetic dyes or harsh chemicals—just vibrant, nature-powered colour.

3. Is it safe for sensitive skin or acne-prone skin?

Absolutely. Our creams are made with gentle, non-comedogenic ingredients like jojoba oil, coconut oil, and olive squalane. No fragrances, essential oils, or irritants—just clean, nourishing colour.

4. Can I use this around my eyes?

Yes. Our formulas are made with safe, food-grade and mineral-based ingredients suitable for the delicate eye area. Just apply with your finger or a soft brush for a natural shimmer or subtle eye tint.

5. Will it dry out my lips or clog my pores?

Not at all. In fact, it’s packed with vitamin E, hyaluronic acid, and green tea extract to hydrate and soothe your skin. It leaves lips soft and cheeks glowy—never greasy or dry.

6. What’s the texture like? Is it matte, glossy, or dewy?

It has a soft balmy texture with a natural, slightly dewy finish. It’s buildable, lightweight, and melts into the skin for a healthy glow—not sticky, oily, or powdery.

7. How long does it last on the skin?

Expect a natural look that lasts 4–6 hours, depending on your skin type. Reapply as needed—our portable tins make touch-ups easy and effortless throughout the day.

8. Is it vegan and cruelty-free?

Yes! We use no animal-derived ingredients (no beeswax, carmine, etc.) and never test on animals. Our colour creams are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and ethical.

9. What’s the shelf life, and how should I store it?

Each tin lasts up to 12 months after opening. Store it in a cool, dry place and avoid leaving it in direct sunlight (especially in hot climates) to maintain freshness and texture.

10. Is the packaging sustainable?

Yes! Our eco tins are fully recyclable, lightweight, and plastic-free. We keep packaging minimal to reduce waste and environmental impact—because your beauty routine shouldn’t cost the Earth.
